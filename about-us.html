Our Story

Sac Tabla Maker is a home-based tabla-making service proudly located in Sacramento, California. The idea began when we noticed a lack of accessible tabla making and reheading services in the local community, leaving many people without reliable support. Inspired by friends who share a deep love for playing tabla—and who faced the same challenges—we took the initiative to respond to that need. What started at home in March 2025 began with offering free tablas to the local Sikh community. The overwhelmingly positive feedback and encouragement that followed gave us the confidence to turn our passion into a business. Today, we provide finished puddas and duggas, professional reheading services, custom orders for custom-scaled tablas, and minor repairs to improve sound quality. Built on love for seva (selfless service) and community, Sac Tabla Maker continues to grow with the support of over 800 followers on Instagram at @sac_tabla_maker.

Founders

Tanvir Singh, a 19-year-old founder based in Sacramento, is the heart behind Sac Tabla Maker. Passionate about tabla making and reheading, Tanvir brings a bright mind and a deep understanding of the craft to every instrument he works on. Rooted in Sikh values and closely connected with the local Sikh community, his work is guided by a strong commitment to service (seva) and giving back. Dedicated to quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction, Tanvir strives to provide high-quality tabla services while supporting the greater community through care, craftsmanship, and tradition.

Keerat Singh, a 17-year-old co-founder based in Sacramento, plays an active role in shaping Sac Tabla Maker’s online presence and community outreach. He manages the brand’s Instagram content and built the SacTablaMaker website, helping bring a local, youth-driven vision to life. Beyond digital work, Keerat occasionally assists with preparing new products and supporting repair work, gaining hands-on experience in traditional craftsmanship. Guided by the values of seva and service, he is passionate about responding to community needs and supporting people through meaningful, accessible work.
